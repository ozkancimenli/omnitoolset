## ‚ö†Ô∏è Areas Requiring Ongoing Monitoring

### 1. Ad Placement Compliance

#### Ads Interfering with Content
- **Current Status:** ‚úÖ COMPLIANT
- **Guidelines:**
  - Ads must not overlay navigational elements
  - Ads must not push content off display
  - Ads must not be on "dead end" screens
  - Ads must not severely interfere with content consumption
- **Current Implementation:**
  - Banner ads placed above and below content (not overlaying)
  - Ads have proper spacing from navigation elements
  - No ads on error pages or "dead end" screens
- **Monitoring:** Review monthly to ensure compliance

#### More Ads Than Content
- **Current Status:** ‚úÖ COMPLIANT
- **Guideline:** Pages must have more publisher content than ads/paid promotional material
- **Current Implementation:**
  - Homepage: Extensive content sections + tool descriptions
  - Tool Pages: Detailed "About This Tool" sections with features and use cases
  - Blog Pages: Full blog articles with substantial content
- **Monitoring:** Review quarterly, especially when adding new ad units

#### Out of Context Ads
- **Current Status:** ‚úÖ COMPLIANT
- **Guideline:** Ads must appear in context with publisher content
- **Current Implementation:**
  - Ads placed within content flow (top, middle, bottom)
  - No ads on background pages
  - No ads outside the display area
- **Monitoring:** Review monthly

## üö® Critical Issues to Address

### 1. Popunder Ads (Better Ads Standards)
**Priority:** HIGH
**Action:** Review and potentially remove popunder ads if they violate Better Ads Standards. Popunders are generally considered intrusive and may violate user experience standards.

**Recommendation:**
- Remove popunder ads or replace with less intrusive formats
- Focus on banner ads and sidebar ads that don't interrupt user experience
- Ensure all ad formats comply with Coalition for Better Ads standards
