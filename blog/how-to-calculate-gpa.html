How to Calculate GPA - Free GPA Calculator

Your Grade Point Average (GPA) is an important metric for academic performance. Our free GPA calculator helps you calculate your GPA accurately for high school or college.

What is GPA?

GPA (Grade Point Average) is a numerical representation of your academic performance, typically on a scale of 0.0 to 4.0. It's calculated by averaging the grade points earned in all courses.

How to Calculate GPA

  1. Visit our GPA Calculator
  2. Enter your course grades (A, B, C, D, F)
  3. Enter credit hours for each course
  4. Select weighted or unweighted GPA
  5. Click "Calculate GPA"

GPA Scale

  • A: 4.0 points (Excellent)
  • B: 3.0 points (Good)
  • C: 2.0 points (Average)
  • D: 1.0 points (Below Average)
  • F: 0.0 points (Failing)

Weighted vs Unweighted GPA

  • Unweighted GPA: All courses count equally (standard scale)
  • Weighted GPA: Honors, AP, or IB courses may count more (e.g., A = 5.0)

GPA Calculation Formula

GPA = Sum of (Grade Points √ó Credit Hours) / Total Credit Hours

Tips for Improving GPA

  • Attend all classes and participate actively
  • Complete assignments on time
  • Study regularly and prepare for exams
  • Seek help when needed (tutoring, office hours)
  • Take challenging courses to improve weighted GPA

Our GPA calculator is free to use and provides accurate calculations for both weighted and unweighted GPAs.
