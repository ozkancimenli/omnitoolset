How to Merge PDF Files - Complete Guide 2024

Published: November 30, 2024

Introduction

Merging PDF files is a common task that many people need to do regularly. Whether you're combining multiple documents for work, school, or personal use, our free online PDF merger makes it easy.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Open the PDF Merger Tool

Navigate to our PDF Merge tool. This tool works entirely in your browser - no software download required.

Step 2: Upload Your PDF Files

Click the upload area or drag and drop your PDF files. You can select multiple files at once. The tool supports up to 10 PDF files at a time.

Step 3: Arrange Files (Optional)

If needed, you can reorder the files by dragging them. The order determines how pages appear in the final merged PDF.

Step 4: Merge and Download

Click the "Merge PDFs" button. The tool will combine all files into one PDF. Once complete, click "Download" to save your merged file.

Tips for Best Results

  • Ensure all PDFs are not password-protected
  • Check file sizes - very large files may take longer
  • Verify the page order before merging
