How to Compress Images Online - Free Image Compressor

Large image files can slow down websites, consume storage space, and make sharing difficult. Our free image compressor helps you reduce file sizes while maintaining acceptable quality.

Why Compress Images?

  • Faster Websites: Smaller images load faster, improving user experience
  • Save Storage: Reduce storage costs and usage
  • Easy Sharing: Smaller files are easier to email or upload
  • Mobile Optimization: Reduce data usage for mobile users

How to Compress Images

  1. Visit our Image Compressor
  2. Upload your image (JPG, PNG, or WebP)
  3. Choose compression quality
  4. Click "Compress Image"
  5. Download the compressed image

Compression Tips

  • Start with medium quality - you can always compress more if needed
  • JPG is better for photos, PNG for graphics with transparency
  • Compare original and compressed versions before saving
  • Keep originals as backup before compressing

All compression happens in your browser - your images are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy.
