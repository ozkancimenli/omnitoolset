How to Generate QR Codes Online - Free QR Code Generator

QR codes are versatile tools for sharing information quickly. Whether you need to share a website URL, contact information, or WiFi credentials, our free QR code generator makes it easy.

What Can You Create QR Codes For?

  • Website URLs: Share links to your website or landing pages
  • Contact Information: vCard format for business cards
  • WiFi Passwords: Easy network sharing
  • Text Messages: Pre-filled SMS or email
  • Plain Text: Any text content

How to Generate a QR Code

  1. Visit our QR Code Generator
  2. Enter your content (URL, text, etc.)
  3. Choose size and error correction level
  4. Click "Generate QR Code"
  5. Download or copy your QR code

QR Code Best Practices

  • Use appropriate error correction level for your use case
  • Test your QR code before printing or sharing
  • Ensure sufficient contrast for scanning
  • Keep QR codes at least 1x1 inch for easy scanning

Our QR code generator is completely free, requires no registration, and works entirely in your browser.
