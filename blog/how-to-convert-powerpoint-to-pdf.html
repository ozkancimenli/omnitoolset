How to Convert PowerPoint to PDF - Free PPTX to PDF Converter

Converting PowerPoint presentations to PDF ensures your slides look the same on any device and can be easily shared or printed. Our free online converter makes this process quick and simple.

Why Convert PowerPoint to PDF?

  • Universal Compatibility: PDFs open on any device without PowerPoint
  • Format Preservation: Maintains slide layouts and formatting
  • Easy Sharing: PDFs are smaller and easier to email
  • Print Ready: Optimized for printing handouts

How to Convert PowerPoint to PDF

  1. Visit our PowerPoint to PDF converter
  2. Upload your PowerPoint file (.ppt or .pptx)
  3. Wait for the conversion to complete
  4. Download your PDF file

Supported PowerPoint Formats

  • PPT - Older PowerPoint format
  • PPTX - Modern PowerPoint format (recommended)

Conversion Tips

  • Review your presentation before conversion
  • Ensure all fonts and images are embedded
  • Check slide layouts and formatting
  • For animations, note that PDFs are static

Common Use Cases

  • Sharing presentations with clients
  • Creating printable handouts
  • Archiving presentations
  • Emailing presentations as attachments
