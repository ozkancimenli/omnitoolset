How to Generate Strong Passwords - Free Password Generator

Strong passwords are essential for protecting your online accounts. Our free password generator helps you create secure, random passwords that are difficult to guess or crack.

Why Use a Password Generator?

  • Security: Random passwords are harder to crack than predictable ones
  • Uniqueness: Generate different passwords for each account
  • Complexity: Include letters, numbers, and symbols automatically
  • Convenience: No need to think of passwords yourself

How to Generate a Strong Password

  1. Visit our Password Generator
  2. Set password length (recommended: 12-16 characters)
  3. Choose character types (uppercase, lowercase, numbers, symbols)
  4. Click "Generate Password"
  5. Copy the generated password

Password Best Practices

  • Use at least 12 characters for strong passwords
  • Include a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
  • Use unique passwords for each account
  • Store passwords in a secure password manager
  • Change passwords regularly, especially after security breaches

Password Strength Tips

  • Avoid dictionary words or common phrases
  • Don't use personal information (names, birthdays)
  • Consider using passphrases for easier memorization
  • Enable two-factor authentication when available

Our password generator works entirely in your browser - passwords are generated locally and never sent to any server.
