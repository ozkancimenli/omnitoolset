Frequently Asked Questions

Is OmniToolset really free?

Yes! All our tools are 100% free to use, with no registration required, no watermarks, and unlimited usage.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely! All processing happens in your browser. Your files never leave your device and are never uploaded to our servers.

Do you store my files?

No. We don't store, upload, or have access to any of your files. Everything is processed locally in your browser.

Yes! We're always adding new tools based on user requests. Feel free to reach out with your suggestions.
