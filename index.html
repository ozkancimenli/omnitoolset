How It Works

When you use an OmniToolset tool, the processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. For example, when you compress a PDF, the file is read on your device, reduced in size using client-side libraries, and the result is downloaded directly to you. We do not store, copy, or transmit your files to our servers. This approach keeps your data private and often makes operations faster than server-based alternatives.

Our site works in all modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) on desktop and mobile. You can use the tools from any device without installing software. We regularly update and test our tools to support the latest formats and to fix bugs reported by users.
